## Quick Start

To begin a new project in SHR Creative Studio:

1. Click the "New Project" button from your dashboard.
2. Enter a project name and brief description.
3. Select a template or start blank.
4. Click "Create" to generate your workspace.
5. Begin adding pages or uploading existing docs.

After setup, you can:
- Organize docs by folders
- Invite collaborators
- Assign permissions

Your project is auto-saved. Return anytime from your dashboard.
