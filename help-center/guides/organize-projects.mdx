---
title: Organizing Projects and Folders
description: Learn to structure your documentation with projects and folders.
---

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create folders">
    ````
    From your project dashboard, click "Add Folder" and name it by topic or team.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Move pages">
    ````
    Drag and drop documentation pages into relevant folders for clarity.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Collaborate">
    ````
    Assign folder access to team members or groups for organized workflows.
    ````
  </Step>
</Steps>
