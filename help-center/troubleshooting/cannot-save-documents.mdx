---
title: Cannot Save Documents
description: How to resolve document saving issues.
---

## Problem
- Document edits aren't saved
- Message: "Save Failed" appears

## Causes
- Lost internet connection
- Browser cache issue

## Solution
<Steps>
  <Step title="Check connection">
    ````bash
    Visit another website to test connectivity.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Refresh browser">
    Try a hard refresh: Ctrl+Shift+R (Windows), Cmd+Shift+R (Mac).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clear cache">
    Clear browser cache and cookies, then re-login.
  </Step>
</Steps>
